Ready to Grow?

Let's scale together. The auto repair industry is consolidating. Don't get left behind - become part of something bigger with strategic M&A services.

Why Growth Makes Sense Now

The automotive aftermarket is undergoing a massive transformation. Here's why expanding now can transform your business.

The Industry is Consolidating

Independent shops face increasing challenges from rising costs, technician shortages, and evolving customer expectations. Meanwhile, consolidated operators with multiple locations enjoy better pricing, stronger branding, and operational efficiencies single-location shops can't match.

Your Foundation is Strong

You built something great. Your shop has a loyal customer base, skilled technicians, and a reputation you've earned over years. Now imagine leveraging that foundation to acquire additional locations and build something truly transformative.

The Profit Potential

Multi-shop operators consistently outperform single-location shops.

Economies of Scale

Better pricing on parts, supplies, and insurance. Negotiate better terms with vendors when you have multiple locations.

Increased Revenue

Cross-sell services across locations and customer bases. A customer who trusts you with one shop will trust you with more.

Technician Development

Shared training programs and career paths that attract and retain top talent. Offer advancement opportunities others can't.

Exit Readiness

A larger, more valuable business when it's time to sell. Multi-location businesses command premium valuations from buyers.

Not Ready to Acquire?

That's okay. There are other paths to growth.

Maybe you're not quite ready to buy another shop - but you might be interested in exploring your options. Perhaps you'd consider a merger with another shop in your area, bringing in a partner, or positioning your business for a future exit that includes your employees or management team.

Whatever your growth aspirations, we can help you explore the possibilities - no pressure, no commitment. We're here to help you make informed decisions about the future of your business.
